Beetroot (Beta Vulgaris)

  1. Common Varieties
  2. Nutritional information
  3. Tags

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the root.

The usual deep purple roots of beetroot are either eaten grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil with vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.
